Survival and Strategy on a Mysterious Island

Committee Overview

This crisis committee will consist of uneventful characters stuck on an island after an unfortunate plane crash. Each delegate has a different self-motive, but they all have to work together to figure out a way off this mysterious island. But because of everyone's self-motive, they all have different ways to get out, and some don't even want to leave. But since getting off the island requires teamwork, delegates will be forced to debate possible methods to get off the mysterious island.

Topic: Escape from the Island

In the year 2027, delegates will be posed with the challenge of getting off of an island after a private jet full of high profile celebrities crashes while fleeing a total world war. Jim, a mysterious passenger on the plane, murders the captain, and passengers fall into total shock, accidentally crashing on an undiscovered piece of land in the mid-Atlantic. Delegates will have to survive while fulfilling their goals. Some need to get off the island and some need to stay and some need to save the world. Will they come together and find purpose, or will they fall into the hands of greed and destruction?
